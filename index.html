Bio

I am a Ph.D. scholar at Atlantic Technological University, Sligo, Ireland, under the supervision of Dr. Saritha Unnikrishnan. My research focuses on 'Generative AI for Non-Invasive MRI Enhancement in Brain Tumor Diagnosis. "Before joining ATU, I worked as a Research Associate at the IML Lab, Information Technology University (ITU), Lahore, Pakistan, on a Google-funded medical imaging project, mentored by Dr. Waqas Sultani and Dr. Mohsen Ali. While working at ITU, my research focused on developing tools for analyzing peripheral blood smear films to enhance automated WBC cell counting with essential explanations, culminating in an oral invited paper at MICCAI 2024 [ArXiv'24]. In addition to this work, I am an accomplished researcher in deep learning and medical imaging, with over five years of research experience and 23+ publications in top-tier conferences and journals, including MICCAI 2024. My expertise spans machine learning, computer vision, and medical image analysis. Beyond research, I serve as an Associate Lecturer and Lab Demonstrator at ATU, Sligo. Additionally, I actively review manuscripts for several leading medical imaging and computer vision journals and conferences.
